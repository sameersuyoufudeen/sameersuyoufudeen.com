About Me

"I am Sameer, a consulting data analyst and social researcher passionate about working with organisations that want to make effective use of their data; from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable your organisation in making evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences. I also am preparing to offer training in Tableau, SQL, R programming, Advanced Excel and Data Literacy very soon. I am available for short-term freelancing projects or on longer-term consulting contracts.

By the way, check out my awesome work".

Work

Here I showcase my most recent project:

Exploratory Study: Tata Nexon EV

Explored data using google sheets, collected from users to see if Fast charging has any connection with occurrence of HV error. The basic theory is that the Fast charging equipment send current in high voltages than the vehicle can accommodate thus going into an error-state. Created a live dashboard using google sheets & report to show my findings.
